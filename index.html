Hello!

I’m Ayodelé. With more than 10 years of experience in interior design, my goal is to help others design the home they’ve always dreamed of.

I offer affordable interior design services for people just like you, who want to live in a beautiful house but just don’t know where to start. True story: clients cried (tears of joy) when I revealed rooms to them, and I loved every minute of it!.

Let me help you create a well-designed home that you want to live in, enjoy with your family, and proudly welcome your friends.

why choose me

finding your style

I take the time to listen, discover, and understand a style you truly love. I’m not here to push my taste onto you.

cost transparency

I let you know how many hours I work on your project and all the costs you should expect to pay for my services—no hidden fees.

savings on products

My close supplier relationships ensure you get unique items for your home at discounted prices, which you won’t see in stores.

how we work

consult

We’ll start with a free phone consultation and/or an in-home consultation. You’ll share your style with me, your expectations, or ask me anything.

concept

This step involves initial concept design, costing, and planning. I’ll provide you with an idea of overall costs based on how many hours I’ll work on your project and estimate a budget for decor and furniture.

source

Once you approve the concept, I’ll start sourcing products for your home. You’ll receive detailed descriptions with images, costs, and dimensions of each item. If you’re satisfied with my proposal, I’ll send you an invoice and make orders with my suppliers.

stlye

Once products are delivered to your home, I’ll come and style the house based on our initial plan. You just need to sit back and see the magic happen.
